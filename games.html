Evil Age

Evil Age is an Unreal Engine 4 game that is an AI simulation of a medieval village being attacked each night by a horde of monsters. Depending on the biomes used to create the village and the actions of the player it will hold more or less night before being decimated.

Check out our game design document

Evil Age use the gameplay ability system which is a plugin to ease the building of abilities like health management, damage, powerful skill, buff and attribute system. It has been used in AAA games such as Paragon, Gears Tactics and Fortnite among others. It cannot be used without a bit of C++. Therefore, this is not an only blueprint project!

This project is still in production! :)

Tools : Unreal Engine, Adobe Photoshop

Developed with Guillaume Haerinck

Sakari

Sakari is a 3D puzzle game in third person based on a time-rewind mechanic made with Unity. he player has the capacity to go back in time, undoing any changes he made to the environment. After the rewind a clone will spawn and redo the last player actions. It has been deployed to PC.

You can check out our technical specification, our functional specification and also some gameplay !

Tools : Unity, Blender, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effect

Developed with Enguerrand De Smet, Jules Fouchy, Guillaume Haerinck and Antoine Libert

Link to the source code and build

A day at ESIPE

A day at ESIPE is a Unity 2D game representing the daily life of an ESIPE student (my education at University). This project allows us to mobilize the creative and computer notions acquired during our training and to promote the school outside. The game is played with keyboard and mouse and is divided into five different levels with an introduction and an ending.

Tools : Unity, Blender, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe After Effect

Developed with Louisa Chikar and Margaux Séguy

Knock Knock - GGJ 2021

Unreal Engine 4 game made for the Global Game Jam 2021. As a key finder, your role is to find keys that have been lost in your client's apartment and the only way to find them fast enough is to break things. Your goal is to break as few things as possible and to find the key as fast as possible. Playable alone or with someone!

Tools : Blender, Unreal Engine 4, Mixamo

Link to the Global Game Jam page

Developed with Guillaume Haerinck
